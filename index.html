Privacy Policy for "Keyword Trends Explorer"

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes the data handling practices of "Keyword Trends Explorer" in relation to the data collected from Pinterest for educational and demonstration purposes.

Data Collection

We collect only publicly available trend data related to keywords from Pinterest. No personal data or identifiable information is collected.

Data Use

The collected data is used solely for the purpose of creating a dashboard that demonstrates data analysis skills. This dashboard may be shared on professional platforms like LinkedIn for educational and career development purposes.

Data Sharing

No collected data is shared with third parties, and it is used exclusively by the creator for the aforementioned purposes.

Security Measures

Appropriate measures are taken to ensure the security and confidentiality of the data used.

Contact Information

For any queries or concerns regarding this privacy policy or data practices, please contact "Keyword Trends Explorer".

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The most current version will always be posted on our website.
